Family Business

In a town a few kilometers from Catania, my family runs three stores. The main store, which is also the largest, is called Sportivo Emozioni Sensazioni and offers clothing for men, women, and children, ranging from sportswear to elegant attire, with a wide assortment of products. The second store, in chronological order, is the Outlet, located in the center of Giarre, which sells unsold stock from the main store. Finally, Pippo Junior is the third store, entirely dedicated to children, with a more focused selection of footwear but also some clothing items to meet the needs of our customers.
